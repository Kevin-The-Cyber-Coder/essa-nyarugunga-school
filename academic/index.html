Academics at ESSA

Excellence in Education | Diverse Programs | Holistic Development

Academic Excellence

Quality Education For Every Student

At ESSA Nyarugunga, we follow the Rwandan national curriculum enhanced with modern teaching methodologies. Our academic programs are designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and practical knowledge.

We offer both Ordinary Level (S1-S3) and Advanced Level (S4-S6) programs with various combinations to suit different career paths.
