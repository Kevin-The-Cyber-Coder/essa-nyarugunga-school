Application Process

Follow these simple steps to join ESSA Nyarugunga

1

Get Application Form

Download the form from our website or collect it from the school administration office.

2

Fill & Submit

Complete the application form with accurate information and attach required documents.

3

Entrance Assessment

Take the entrance examination (English, Mathematics, General Knowledge).

4

Admission Decision

Receive admission notification within 7-10 working days.

5

Enrollment

Complete registration, pay fees, and join our academic community.

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • Completion of Primary 6 (for O-Level)
  • Completion of S3 (for A-Level)
  • Minimum 70% average in previous year
  • Passing score on entrance exam

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (2 copies)
  • Last 2 years' Report Cards
  • 4 Passport Photos
  • Medical Certificate
  • Parent/Guardian ID copies

Important Dates

  • Applications Open: January 10, 2026
  • Deadline: September 30, 2026
  • Entrance Exams: Weekly on Saturdays
  • Classes Begin: October 15, 2026

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the application process start?

Applications open on January 10, 2026, and remain open until September 30, 2026. We recommend applying early as seats are limited.

Is there an entrance examination?

Yes, all applicants must take an entrance exam covering English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge. Exams are held every Saturday at 9 AM.

Can I pay fees in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Please contact the finance office to discuss installment arrangements.

Is accommodation available?

Currently, ESSA Nyarugunga is a day school. We do not offer boarding facilities. However, we can assist with recommendations for nearby hostels.

What is the school uniform policy?

All students are required to wear the official ESSA Nyarugunga uniform. Uniforms can be purchased from the school store or approved vendors.

How do I check my application status?

You can check your application status by contacting the admissions office via phone or email. We will also notify you via SMS/email.
